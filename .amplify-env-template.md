## After First Deployment

Once you get your Amplify URL (https://main.xxxxx.amplifyapp.com), add:

```
NEXT_PUBLIC_APP_URL=https://main.xxxxx.amplifyapp.com
```

Then redeploy to apply the change.

## Deployment Instructions

1. Go to: https://console.aws.amazon.com/amplify/home?region=us-east-1
2. Click "New app" → "Host web app"
3. Choose "GitHub" → Authorize
4. Select: Rappin-hAcker18/Devops-labs (branch: main)
5. Build settings will auto-detect from amplify.yml
6. Click "Advanced settings" → Add all environment variables above
7. Click "Save and deploy"
8. Wait ~3-5 minutes for build to complete
9. Get your URL and update NEXT_PUBLIC_APP_URL
10. Redeploy with new URL

## Webhook Setup (After Deployment)

Update your Stripe webhook endpoint to:
```
https://your-amplify-url/api/webhooks/stripe
```
